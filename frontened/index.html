How It Works

Three Steps to Smarter Forecasting

01 📊

Data Collection

Historical food order data including prices, promotions, and fulfillment center information is ingested and cleaned automatically.

02 ⚙️

Feature Engineering

Lag features and exponentially weighted moving averages capture temporal dependencies and seasonal patterns for each SKU.

03 🤖

Forecasting Models

Machine learning models such as Random Forest, CatBoost, and LightGBM are ensembled to predict future food demand with high accuracy.
