About This Project

Smart Food Demand Forecasting

Developed as a Machine Learning & Deep Learning based forecasting system for improved supply chain optimization.

🎯

What We Do

Smart Food Demand Forecasting is a machine learning and deep learning–based system designed to accurately predict food demand in meal delivery services. Since food products are perishable, incorrect demand prediction can result in overstocking, wastage, or shortages. Our platform provides intelligent forecasting solutions to improve supply chain planning and operational efficiency.

⚠️

The Problem

Existing platforms such as BlueYonder, SAP IBP, Oracle Demantra, and Infor Demand Management rely largely on traditional statistical models. These models often fail to capture complex temporal patterns in meal demand, leading to inefficiencies in inventory management.

🔬

Our Approach

This project proposes a time series–based forecasting approach using regressor-based machine learning and deep learning models. The system is trained using the Food Demand Forecasting dataset released by Genpact, which includes:

  • Historical weekly meal order data
  • Fulfillment center information
  • Pricing and promotional details
  • Categorical attributes

Temporal dependencies are captured by formulating the forecasting problem as a supervised regression task using lag-based features and Exponentially Weighted Moving Averages (EWMA).

🤖

Models Implemented

Random Forest
CatBoost
LightGBM

Experimental results demonstrate that ensemble machine learning models such as Random Forest, CatBoost, and LightGBM provide accurate and stable food demand predictions.

✅

To build intelligent, data-driven systems that reduce food wastage, enhance operational efficiency, and promote sustainable supply chain management.
